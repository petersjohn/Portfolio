Get to know me

I am a life-long learner. My journey to become a developer has taken a long and circuitous route to get to where I am now. At an early age, I learned to code in BASIC on my dad's Tandy 1000, and even used that knowledge to write programs to help with my algebra homework on the venerable Ti-81 graphing calculator. Then after a short lived stint in traditional university, I took a computer networking technology program at a local community college and I absolutely loved it. That experience reignited my passion for technology. It led me to finish my education and land my first professional role at one of the world's larget healthcare technology companies. When the opportunity came to "skill up", ElevenFifty Academy was there. My experience the Software Development course is an amazing addition to my skillsets, and has provided a new launchpad for continued growth. I would love to bring my experience to your organization and create new value and experiences along the way.

Career Highlights

Cerner - Support Analyst

Tier 1 and 2 support analyst for the Cerner FirstNet emergency medicine application. I was tasked with supporting clients all across the globe. In the two years that I was there I earned 18 Client Service Awards.

Indiana University Health - Clinical IS Architect

Promoted from analyst to Clinical IS Architect within 8 years. My core focus the last few years was developing gap-fill functionalities and extending our clinical IS capabilities. In this time, I created custom applications using Cerner Command Language (CCL), devised algorithms that could alert other departments if the emergency department was getting close to full utilization, saved millions of dollars for the organization by creating automated method to capture missed infusion charges and many other projects that created value for the organization.

ALKU/Holland Square Group - Consultant

Created custom development across a wide variety of healthcare IT needs. Helped clients automate processes, reduced service backlogs and assisted in helping clients design workflows to streamline clinical care and shorten revenue cycle.

Cerner - Associate Lead Software Engineer

Design and implement development projects to fit client requirements. Currently resposible for approval of technical requirements, scoping project hours, assessing technical feasiblity, and reviewing code for other engineering resources. I also somehow find time to develop as well!
